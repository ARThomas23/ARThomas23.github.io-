Capriola is a sans-serif typeface whose unique style draws upon forms seen in handwriting and italic types. Skeletons of the most characteristic glyphs are inspired by quick handwriting and based on a single hand movement (G,a,g,k,e).
Capriola ambitiously seeks to push the boundaries of originality in the genre without losing legibility.
